Our Story

Little Lemon was founded in 2020 by the Antonios family, bringing Mediterranean cuisine with a modern twist to the heart of Chicago. What started as a small family restaurant has grown into a beloved local establishment, known for its fresh ingredients and innovative dishes.

Our Philosophy

At Little Lemon, we believe in three core principles:

  • Fresh Ingredients: We source our ingredients from local farmers and markets daily.
  • Traditional Recipes: Our dishes are based on family recipes passed down through generations.
  • Modern Innovation: We add contemporary twists to classic Mediterranean dishes.
